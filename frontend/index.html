How it works

From hand shape to typed letter

01

Capture

Your browser streams webcam frames over a WebSocket to a FastAPI backend — lightweight enough to run several times a second.

02

Track

MediaPipe Hands locates 21 landmark points on your hand per frame — fingertips, knuckles, and wrist — regardless of background or lighting.

03

Normalize

Landmark coordinates are made translation- and scale-invariant, so the same sign is recognized whether your hand is close, far, or off-center.

04

Classify

A trained multilayer perceptron (MLP) maps the 63-value landmark vector to one of 28 classes: 26 letters, space, and delete.

05

Stabilize & type

Predictions are smoothed across frames to avoid flicker, then committed letter-by-letter into typed text — with autocorrect and audio feedback on completed words.
