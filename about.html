About

The person behind this project

Hi, I'm Charan Teja

I'm an aspiring Data Engineer interested in computer vision and accessible technology. NoTouchTyping started as a way to combine real-time hand tracking with a small, fast classifier — and turn it into something genuinely usable rather than just a notebook experiment.

This project covers the full pipeline: collecting and training on MediaPipe hand-landmark data, building a low-latency FastAPI + WebSocket backend for real-time inference, and shipping a browser frontend that needs nothing installed on the user's end.
